Tacos

Description

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small hand-sized corn or wheat tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling and eaten by hand.

Ingredients

  • 2 (15 ounce) cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • 8 taco bells

Steps

  1. Place chickpeas in a saucepan over medium heat. Add water and taco seasoning. Cook and stir until thoroughly heated, 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from heat. Mash mixture to crush chickpeas.
  2. Divide chickpea filling evenly among taco shells.
